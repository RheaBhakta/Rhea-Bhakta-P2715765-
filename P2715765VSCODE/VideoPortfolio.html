Rhea Bhakta: My University Expereince

As part of my university course, I was tasked to create an video advertisement. I chose to produce an advert for Lucozade Sport, the ultimate performance drink for athletes. My advert showcases the power of Lucozade Sport to enhance athletic performance. My advert has a focus on how Lucozade Sport helps athletes get inspired and help sustain energy during intense training sessions. The advert centres around a singular athlete as he pushes though his limits to achieve his goals with the help of Lucozade Sport. Through stunning visuals and an inspiring message, my advert aims to showcase how Lucozade Sport can be the perfect companion for anyone striving to reach their full potential during their workouts.
